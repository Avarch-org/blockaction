FAQ

Why you need a BlockAction Digital Wallet?

BlockAction provides Secured and simplified solution to blockchain transaction. We offer a free digital asset wallet and an easy to use interface for mainstream users. Other reasons to choose us are:

You don’t need to download any application on desktop or mobile to create wallet or perform transaction.
  1. You don’t want to keep your cryptocurrency at the exchange as it is not safe and vulnerable to cyber-attacks.
  2. Exchanges have different withdrawal policies and may require you to wait for a certain amount of time or confirm the withdrawal via an email link before processing it.
  3. Password selected and Private key generated are ‘for your eyes only’. BlockAction does not store either of them because you want to keep your cryptocurrency (Ether and Bitcoin) safe with you as the owner of private key of your wallet.
  4. All BlockAction transactions are publicly visible on the blockchain.
  5. You can view real time transaction status.
  6. You can send or request receive payments in cryptocurrency without disclosing your identity.
  7. It is fast and secured.
  8. You can access your wallet anytime, anywhere with any device (mobile, tablet, computers) without downloading the entire blockchain.
Do I need to have understanding of blockchain to create wallet or perform transaction?
BlockAction provide user friendly interface to create wallet and perform transactions. A user with zero knowledge of blockchain technology can also create wallet and send or request currency.
How safe is my wallet?
BlockAction takes security very seriously. Your password and private key are encrypted. Decryption happens client-side, so we never have access to your wallet.
Can I access BlockAction.io from my mobile phone?
Yes, BlockAction is multi device supported. You can access your wallet from any device without downloading the entire blockchain.
Can I use BlockAction to perform transaction on an existing wallet?
Yes, BlockAction supports transactions for new wallet as well as existing wallets created from another website.
How secure is my private key and transaction on BlockAction.io?
Users can generate wallet offline and reconnect after saving private key. So, it is safe. Also, all our transactions happen on SSL layer making it completely secured.
